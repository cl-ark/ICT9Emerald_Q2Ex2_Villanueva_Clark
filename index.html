Best Places to Visit in the Summer


Baguio

Known as the summer capital of the Philippines, people love to go to Baguio whenever temperatures are too hot to handle.

Due to it's cold climate, with temperatures being as cold as our indoor aircons, it is a suitable tourist spot for those looking to cool off.




Boracay

Being another popular tourist spot, Boracay brings out the true natural beauties of the Philippines. With it's white sands and pristine

waters, people all over the world knows the name of the little party island.
